DATE Cycle 2 Year 2 Grant Application Available on Egrants

The DATE Cycle 2 Year 2 Application, due on April 7, 2011, is a continuation grant of the current DATE grant that you have in your district now. This means that if your district would like to continue to have the DATE program in your district for the 2011-2012 school year, then you must complete the DATE Year 2 application by April 7.

Your district may use the same DATE plan that it has now, during the 2010-2011 school year. However, if your district would like to make any changes to its current plan, or would like to create a completely new DATE plan, then you may also re-design your program and fill out the Year 2 application with new program information.

Please don't hesitate to contact technical assistance with any questions or concerns or to assist with program design questions and to review your grant application. We are here to assist with this process so please feel free to contact us!
