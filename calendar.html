Spring Payout Workshops

Throughout the spring, technical assistance and the regional service centers will be hosting workshops on payout. These workshops are strongly recommended for grant managers, payroll, human resource, data personnel, campus leaders, and media relations staff. They will be held at the regional service centers and it is strongly advised that you bring a laptop to the workshop. Contact your ESC representative for more information about the workshop in your region. Here's the schedule of workshops. Note: For the regions marked ISV, the contact has elected to conduct and individual site visit instead of a group workshop. Please contact your ESC to make arrangements.
