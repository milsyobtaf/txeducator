Participants left this workshop with a sense of major challenges and solutions for each aspect and a draft of an implementation action plan.

2010 D.A.T.E. Summer Institute (DSI)

Both Cycle 1 and Cycle 2 districts gathered in San Antonio for a three-day institute from June 28-30, 2010, in which districts shared best practices, challenges and solutions, and worked together to plan for the 2010-2011 school year. Resources here include highlights from panel discussions, keynote presentations, handouts from district-led breakout sessions, and activities to help plan for implementation.

Implementation Overview

These documents will provide guidance on the overall structure of implementation. District planning committees should familiarize themselves with this section before the start of the implementation year in order to prepare for the multitude of challenges that implementation can present.

Challenges & Solutions

Based on our experiences working with Texas districts and experiences reported from districts implementing strategic compensation nationwide, Technical Assistance has prepared a series of "Challenges and Solutions" documents covering all aspects of implementation. Not only will these documents offer specific solutions to common problems, but they also harken back to the general principles of implementation outlined in the overview materials.
