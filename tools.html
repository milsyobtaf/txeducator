Tools & Resources

Comprehensive Archive of Technical Assistance Resources

This section contains all of best resources that Technical Assistance has created and gathered. Here you will find all of the handouts, powerpoint presentations, and select videos from our recent workshops and conferences; tools and resources for each aspect of D.A.T.E. program design and implementation; model programs, our unique performance measure database; the latest research on strategic compensation; and the archive of our monthly newsletters.

If there is any resource that you cannot find here or feel that we should be providing, please contact us.

Webinar on Programmatic and Budgetary Amendments

This webinar covered the procedures and rationales for submitting programmatic and budgetary amendments. You can download the basic presentation here. We will be posting a recorded version of the webinar with full audio and audience participation soon.

Fall 2010 Cycle 2 Implementation Workshop

This workshop, conducted at almost every ESC in Texas, provided an interactive overview of each aspect of implementation: program management, program monitoring, and communication. Participants left this workshop with a sense of major challenges and solutions for each aspect and a draft of an implementation action plan.

2010 D.A.T.E. Summer Institute (DSI)

Both Cycle 1 and Cycle 2 districts gathered in San Antonio for a three-day institute from June 28-30, 2010, in which districts shared best practices, challenges and solutions, and worked together to plan for the 2010-2011 school year. Resources here include highlights from panel discussions, keynote presentations, handouts from district-led breakout sessions, and activities to help plan for implementation.
